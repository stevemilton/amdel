The Journey

Past, Present & Future

Past

A Career Defined by Elite Service

Ant Middleton's journey is defined by a level of military achievement reached by very few. He is among a rare elite to have served in three of the British Armed Forces' most demanding branches: the Royal Marines, the 9 Parachute Squadron Royal Engineers, and the Special Boat Service (SBS) — the UK's most secretive and elite naval special forces unit. During his time with the SBS, Ant operated at the highest level of global security, honing his skills as a point man and sniper in some of the world's most hostile environments.

As a successful businessman, Ant is continuing his development in the field of Human Performance Entertainment. In 2026, Ant will debut a new broadcast series on YouTube, and will unveil a new broadcast format.
